Welcome to Quokkaroo

The momentum
of a new life.

We help you bridge the uncertain waters of migration — finding a job, upskilling if needed, and settling into your new life in Australia or New Zealand.

To migrate you need to think about everything from getting a job, to which visa to get, to deciding which Australian city to move to and how to settle in. That's us in a nutshell — an Australian skilled migration business with a focus on bringing skilled migrants to Australia to fill acute skills gaps, and to give migrants the opportunity of a new and better life.

How it works

One decision tips
everything into motion.

  1. 01

    Consult

    A 45-minute consultation with Dr Chamonix Terblanche, Registered Migration Agent (MARN 2318272). Fixed fee $420 incl. GST, with a written summary and fact sheets afterwards.

  2. 02

    Visa strategy

    Preliminary advice on your migration and sponsorship options — the visa criteria, the process and realistic government fee estimates.

  3. 03

    Get hired

    We match your skills to Australian employers with acute skills gaps — and train you up with accredited courses where it helps.

  4. 04

    Relocate

    Visa management and relocation support for you and your family — flights, paperwork, even the fur babies.

  5. 05

    Settle in

    Resettlement support and a 10,000-strong migrant community, so your first year Down Under feels like home.
